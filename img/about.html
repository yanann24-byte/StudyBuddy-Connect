Our Background

StudyBuddy Connect was founded in **2023** by a group of former educators and university students who saw a gap in traditional tutoring models. We realized that many students primarily needed help organizing their study time, understanding homework requirements, and developing strong long-term study habits, rather than just solving isolated problems.
